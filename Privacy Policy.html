Privacy Policy for ShoeStop
1. Information We Collect
We collect information like your name, contact details, payment information, and sometimes, your shoe preferences. This helps us process your orders and provide better services.
2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used to process your purchases, communicate with you about orders, and improve our services. Sometimes, we might send you promotions or updates about new shoes.
3. Protection of Your Information
We take steps to keep your information safe. Our systems have security measures in place to prevent unauthorized access or leaks of your details.
4. Sharing Your Information
We don’t sell your information to anyone. Sometimes, we might share it with trusted partners who help us deliver orders or improve our services.
5. Cookies and Tracking
We use cookies to improve your shopping experience on our website. These cookies help remember your preferences and tailor the site to suit your needs.
